About My Education

My School

Sacred Heart Convent School,Mathura

Sacred Heart Convent Higher Secondary School was started in 1958 by Congregation of Carmelite religious as the first English Medium School of Mathura District. Today it is a full fledged institution which runs classes from Nursery to Std. XII, and is affiliated to the Council for the Indian Certificate of Secondary Examinations, New Delhi with code UP069. It has always enjoyed a good reputation but in recent times it has excelled in various fields and has surpassed all previous records in trying to attain its aims and objectives. The credit cannot go to any one person alone but above all it goes to our leaders, our most Reverent Principal, Sister Floripe. Sister Floripe took the reigns of the schools in April 2012. Since then the school has continued to flourish.
